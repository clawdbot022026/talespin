TaleSpin is a collaborative storytelling platform where every story splits into infinite parallel universes, written by the community. It combines the creativity of Wattpad with the branching logic of Git.

## üí° The Core Concept

Unlike linear platforms (Reddit, Twitter), TaleSpin treats stories as **Trees (Graphs)**.
*   **Genesis Node:** The starting point (e.g., "The billionaire spilled soup on his boss.").
*   **The Branching:** Users don't just comment; they **continue** the story.
    *   *Path A:* "He apologized."
    *   *Path B:* "He bought the company."
*   **The Multiverse:** Multiple versions of the story exist simultaneously. Readers choose which timeline to follow.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Mechanics & Rules

### 1. The Checkpoint System
*   **Constraint:** Users write in small chunks (Checkpoints). Max **280 characters** or **1 Paragraph**.
*   **Immutability:** Once a node is posted, it cannot be edited, only branched from.

### 2. Branching Logic
*   At any checkpoint, a user can:
    *   **Vote:** Support an existing continuation (making it "Canon").
    *   **Branch:** Write a *new* continuation (creating a divergent timeline).
*   **The Canon Lock:** The branch with the most votes becomes the "Main Timeline" (visually thicker line), but "Alternative Timelines" are always accessible.

### 3. Gamification
*   **Canon Points:** Earn points when users vote for your branch.
*   **Badges:**
    *   üèóÔ∏è **The Architect:** Started a story with 100+ branches.
    *   üå™Ô∏è **The Twister:** Wrote a plot twist that changed the canon path.
    *   üèÅ **The Closer:** Wrote a highly-voted ending.
